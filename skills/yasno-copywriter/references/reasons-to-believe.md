# Reasons To Believe

Use these proof points carefully and only when relevant.

## Core proof points

1. Yasno helps users choose a suitable psychologist and does not leave them alone with the choice.
2. The service works with approaches that are considered established and effective.
3. Every psychologist is personally selected, documents are checked, and ongoing supervision exists.
4. Customer care includes specialists with psychotherapy education.
5. A large number of people have already used Yasno for support.
6. Yasno has deep expertise in how therapy and the psyche work, reflected in its media and education.
7. Yasno is an early and major player in online psychotherapy in Russia, founded by psychologists.

## How to use proof points

Use them to:

- reduce anxiety around the first step
- support trust
- explain why the service is reliable
- add concrete grounding to marketing copy

Do not use them as:

- chest-beating
- inflated claims
- pressure tactics
