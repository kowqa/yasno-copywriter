# Bot Examples

These examples are drawn from Yasno webinar and course bot flows in Notion.

Use them to understand sequence logic, microcopy tone, and the balance between clarity and warmth.

## Example 1. Webinar registration with immediate value

Sources:

- `чат-бот Самооценка`
- `чат-бот Эмоции`
- `чат-бот Депрессия`

Task:

- registration
- bonus delivery

Why it works:

- opens with the date, time, and topic immediately
- explains the value of registration in one simple sentence
- asks for one field at a time
- delivers a guide or bonus right after the user completes the flow

Reusable patterns:

- `Чтобы прислать ссылку на вебинар и отправить вам гайд, нужно пройти регистрацию`
- `Пожалуйста, напишите, как вас зовут`
- `Оставьте, пожалуйста, ваш email`
- `Спасибо за ответы!`

Do not copy blindly:

- the exact legal wording or event framing if the funnel has different compliance needs

## Example 2. Reminder sequence that does not feel robotic

Sources:

- `чат-бот Самооценка`
- `чат-бот Эмоции`
- `чат-бот Депрессия`
- `чат-бот Расставание`

Task:

- reminder
- attendance activation

Why it works:

- reminders are timed around real decision points:
  - day before
  - same day
  - one hour before
  - at the start
- the text is short and predictable
- some reminders add useful context instead of repeating the same phrase

Reusable patterns:

- `Вебинар уже завтра`
- `Сегодня в 19:00`
- `Через час`
- short bullet list of what will be discussed
- `Подключиться`

- event preparation

- it recognizes that the topic leaves people with questions

## Example 4. Post-webinar transition into therapy

Sources:

- `чат-бот Расставание`
- `чат-бот Депрессия`

Task:

- promo
- service bridge

Why it works:

- starts from gratitude, not sales pressure
- names what the event helped clarify
- offers therapy as support for the next stage
- keeps the CTA direct and legible

Reusable patterns:

- `Спасибо, что пришли на вебинар`
- `Надеемся, нам удалось...`
- `Психолог поможет...`
- `Подобрать психолога`

Warning:

- if the message contains a promo code or legal disclaimer, keep the commercial block visually separate from the supportive body

## Cross-example conclusions

What Yasno bot copy tends to do well:

- reduces friction without becoming sterile
- uses warmth sparingly and effectively
- organizes the funnel around one action per message
- keeps useful editorial value even inside promo sequences
- moves from event -> recording -> next support step with clean logic

What to preserve in new bot copy:

- operational clarity
- short message rhythm
- emotionally accurate phrasing
- explicit buttons and asks
- a soft service voice that never interferes with the action
