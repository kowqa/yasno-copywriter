# VK Examples

These examples are drawn from Yasno VK planning pages and format notes.

Use them to understand how Yasno adapts psychological content for a faster, more interactive social feed.

## Example 1. Recognize-yourself hook

Source:

- `идеи VK`

Task:

- engagement
- recognition-driven post

Why it works:

- it starts from a concrete everyday behavior
- the reader can identify themselves immediately
- the format is naturally shareable and commentable

Reusable patterns:

- begin with `Это я...`
- name a familiar difficulty in ordinary language
- keep the opening socially recognizable, not abstract

## Example 2. Practical exercises post

Source:

- `практики`

Task:

- practical
- educational

Why it works:

- it gives the reader something usable right away
- instructions are short and literal
- the post does not over-explain the exercise
- the practical block stays grounded in self-observation

Reusable patterns:

- start with one sentence of framing
- give 1-3 concrete exercises
- write the steps plainly
- end with an invitation to notice feelings or reactions

Why it works:

- the copy serves the clip rather than competing with it

## Cross-example conclusions

What Yasno VK tends to do well:

- uses recognition as the entry point
- favors interaction over passive admiration
- translates psychology into concrete social situations
- supports clips, polls, and recurring live formats
- keeps practical content simple and usable

What to preserve in new VK writing:

- fast readability
- concrete openings
- explicit format logic
- one action per post
- platform-aware simplicity without losing the brand’s psychological depth
