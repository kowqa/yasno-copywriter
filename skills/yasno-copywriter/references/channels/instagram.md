# Instagram

## Role of the channel

Instagram usually needs quick emotional readability, visual compatibility, and a strong opening.

## Priorities

- fast hook
- short paragraphs
- visual rhythm
- one clear idea
- high save/share value

## Good formats

- carousel headlines
- short body with a clear emotional insight
- compact educational breakdown
- comments prompt if needed

## Avoid

- walls of text
- jargon-heavy explanations
- abstract intros
- fake intimacy
