# VK

## Role of the channel

VK texts should stay readable and direct, often with slightly stronger informational clarity.

In Yasno, VK is not just a repost destination. The planning docs treat it as a more algorithmic, interaction-heavy channel where the text should recognize the reader fast and work well with clips, polls, comments, and short posts.

## Priorities

- clear structure
- practical readability
- honest title
- low jargon
- easy scanning

## How Yasno tends to use VK

From the VK planning pages, the channel is built around:

- short clips
- short posts
- interactive formats
- practical exercises
- polls
- live streams
- situation breakdowns

Compared with Telegram, VK should usually be:

- faster
- more recognizable on first read
- more interaction-oriented
- more explicit in format and prompt

## What the platform logic changes

The VK notes are unusually explicit about distribution:

- clips matter more than long posts
- people in frame tend to perform better than pure animation
- polls count as meaningful interaction
- regular live streams can become a habit format

That means copy for VK should not assume deep reading by default.

It should:

- get to the point fast
- invite one simple action
- make the format obvious
- work well in short attention windows

## VK format families in Yasno

### Family A. Recognize-yourself post

This is one of the clearest platform insights in the notes.

Good VK topics often begin with:

- `Это я...`
- a concrete social situation
- a recognizable conflict
- a named everyday difficulty

Examples of the shape:

- `Это я откладываю важные дела на потом`
- `Это я не знаю, как попросить повышение`

### Family B. Interactive post

This includes:

- polls
- comment prompts
- quizzes
- `choose your path`
- situation breakdowns

The text should help the user act right away, not admire the writing.

### Family C. Practical post

Seen in the `практики` page.

This format works when the post contains:

- a short explanation
- 1-3 usable exercises
- direct, literal instructions

The tone can stay warm, but must remain operational.

### Family D. Clip-first text

VK planning strongly favors clips.

Text for this format should:

- support the spoken or visual content
- open fast
- keep the wording concrete
- avoid dense theoretical build-up

### Family E. Live and recurring interaction

The channel notes suggest recurring live streams and habitual audience interaction.

This means the text should sometimes behave like programming copy:

- announce the format
- explain why to join
- make timing and benefit obvious

## Topic logic in VK

The newer `VK 2.0.` pages show a strong bias toward:

- adolescence
- identity
- relationships with parents
- first romantic relationships
- emotional storms
- belonging
- self-definition
- social comparison
- loneliness

This suggests an important rule:

- VK can carry psychologically serious ideas, but they need to be translated into concrete age-relevant language and recognizable situations

## Openings that work well

Good VK openings often do one of these:

- name a common experience immediately
- pose a direct question
- invite a choice
- frame a relatable dilemma
- signal the interactive format in the first line

Examples of the shape:

- `Почему подросткам важно быть принятыми сверстниками?`
- `Как жить с родительскими ожиданиями и своими мечтами?`
- `Как ты справляешься с тревогой?`

## What makes Yasno VK strong

- the reader recognizes themselves fast
- the format is obvious at a glance
- the post leads naturally to comment, poll, clip, or live
- psychological ideas are translated into concrete social language
- practical posts remain usable, not ornamental

## What to avoid

- overbuilt literary intros
- importing Telegram density unchanged
- hiding the interaction prompt too low in the post
- abstract theory before recognition
- forcing seriousness where play or interaction would work better
- treating every VK post like a long caption

## Avoid

- over-designed literary openings
- complexity for complexity's sake
