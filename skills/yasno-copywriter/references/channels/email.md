# Email

## Role of the channel

Email is intimate and intentional. It should reward the open.

## Priorities

- honest subject line
- strong first screen
- one main action
- measured warmth
- clean CTA

## Default output

When writing email, include:

- 3 subject line options
- preview text
- main body
- CTA options

## Avoid

- spammy urgency
- all-caps energy
- fake scarcity
- overexplaining the obvious
