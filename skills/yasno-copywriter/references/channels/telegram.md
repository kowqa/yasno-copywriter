# Telegram

## Role of the channel

Telegram allows more depth, tone, and personality than most social channels.

## Priorities

- strong opening thought
- clear narrative voice
- depth without heaviness
- readable paragraph rhythm
- sense that the reader gained a new lens

## Good formats

- provocative question
- reframing
- mini-essay
- list with a clear point of view

## Avoid

- clickbait
- vague inspiration
- overlong preambles
